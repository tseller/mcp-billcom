Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

mcp-billcom is a private internal tool that connects to QuickBooks Online and BILL Spend & Expense (Divvy) APIs. It is not a public-facing application and is used solely by its owner.

Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. It accesses QuickBooks Online data solely to provide read access to the authorized account holder.

Data Use

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used only to respond to queries made by the authorized user of this tool. No data is retained beyond the scope of a single request.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party.
